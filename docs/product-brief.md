# Product Brief

This is the canonical product brief for Neo as one consolidated product. It should be used by design, image prompt, and future implementation planning work.

## One-Line Description

Neo is an AI-powered WhatsApp commerce operating system for Nigerian SMEs that helps sellers reply customers, capture orders, verify payments, review receipts, manage follow-ups, coordinate delivery, remember customers, and grow sales from one mobile-first command center.

## Target User

Nigerian WhatsApp-first SME owners and trusted staff who sell through informal chats, voice notes, product screenshots, bank transfers, delivery coordination, and polite local customer language.

## Problem Solved

Neo turns messy WhatsApp selling into one calm operating system. It helps the business see urgent chats, approve AI replies, create orders, review receipt screenshots safely, coordinate delivery, manage custom jobs, follow up unpaid customers, remember customer context, understand performance, and delegate work safely.

## Unified Product Shape

Neo is one product with connected modules:

- Public marketing, demo, pricing, contact sales, help, legal, and trust pages.
- Authentication and onboarding.
- Dashboard and Today Command Center.
- WhatsApp Inbox, Conversation Detail, and customer context.
- Neo Assistant, suggested replies, AI approvals, guardrails, knowledge, training examples, playground, and AI logs.
- Product Catalog, inventory, categories, variants, imports, and WhatsApp catalog sync.
- Orders, order detail, draft orders, payment states, fulfillment states, cancellations, and refunds.
- Payments, provider links, transactions, manual receipts, receipt review, payouts, and payment settings.
- Delivery and logistics, including zones, riders, waybills, failed deliveries, and proof.
- Customers and CRM, including profiles, segments, imports, notes, and blocked customers.
- Workflows and custom job cards for tailoring, food, logistics, real estate, beauty, service bookings, and other SME pipelines.
- Follow-ups, abandoned orders, and lead recovery.
- Broadcasts and WhatsApp templates.
- Analytics, reports, and daily summaries.
- Team, roles, permissions, activity logs, notifications, settings, billing, and internal admin monitoring.

## MVP Product Shape

The MVP is the first mobile-first slice of the same operating system. It should focus on the daily actions that prove the unified product loop:

- Complete setup enough for Neo to answer correctly.
- See what needs attention today.
- Review customer conversations.
- Approve or edit AI draft replies.
- Capture order details from chat.
- Review receipt screenshots before confirming payment.
- Send polite follow-ups to unpaid or silent customers.
- Maintain basic customer memory and settings.

## Non-Goals

These are not separate products and they are not rejected from Neo. They are simply outside the first build slice.

- Public marketing site
- Full admin console
- Full billing portal
- Advanced analytics reports
- Broadcast campaigns
- Fully autonomous payment confirmation
- Full workflow builder
- Final technology stack decision

## Product Principles

- Assist first, automate wisely.
- Human approval is required for sensitive actions.
- Never confirm manual bank transfers from screenshots alone.
- Respect Nigerian selling patterns and customer language.
- Keep mobile screens thumb-friendly and calm.
- Do not make the app feel like a compressed desktop dashboard.
- Make every status clear enough for a busy owner to act quickly.
