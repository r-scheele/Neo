# Screen Illustration Prompts

Use these prompts for feature-specific illustrations. They should support the screen without dominating the primary action.

## Setup Checklist Illustration

```md
Create a screen illustration for the Setup Checklist screen in Neo.

App context:
Neo is a mobile-first AI-powered WhatsApp commerce operating system for Nigerian SMEs. It helps sellers reply customers, capture orders, verify payments, review receipts safely, coordinate delivery, manage workflows, remember customers, recover sales, and stay in control.

Screen context:
This illustration appears during first-time setup, where the user is completing business profile, business type, WhatsApp status, AI tone, payment rules, delivery zones, and products.

Style reference:
Use the approved Neo style anchor for deep forest green, warm ivory, soft beige, muted gold, terracotta, charcoal, soft upper-left lighting, subtle paper texture, and premium editorial flat illustration.

Illustration concept:
A tidy stack of setup tiles represented as abstract objects: business card, chat connection, tone slider, receipt rule, delivery pin, and product card. Show them as calm organized steps, with no readable labels.

Requirements:
- Transparent background.
- No text, letters, numbers, or fake UI.
- Clear at small mobile sizes.
- Gentle warm shadows.
- Does not imply every setup step is already complete.
- Leaves room for app UI copy and buttons.

Avoid:
- No robots or neon AI.
- No busy dashboard.
- No exact app screenshot.
- No cluttered checklist with fake text.

Output:
Save as illustration-setup-checklist.png.
```
