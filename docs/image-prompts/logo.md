# Logo Prompt

Use after the style anchor is approved. Generate the transparent logo mark first, then the app icon source.

## Transparent Logo Mark

```md
Create a mobile app logo mark for Neo, an AI-powered WhatsApp commerce operating system for Nigerian SMEs.

Style reference:
Use the approved Neo style anchor for palette, geometry, simplicity, softness, and calm premium mood. Preserve deep forest green, warm ivory, muted gold, terracotta warmth, and charcoal balance. Do not copy the exact style-anchor composition.

Logo concept:
A simple geometric mark combining three ideas: a calm chat bubble, a folded receipt, and a subtle leaf/check shape that suggests verified commerce and human judgment. The mark should feel wise, practical, and trustworthy, not flashy.

Requirements:
- Simple geometric mark.
- Recognizable at 48px.
- No text, letters, words, numbers, or initials.
- No WhatsApp logo imitation.
- No robot, brain, sparkles, lightning, or neon AI symbols.
- Works on light and dark backgrounds.
- Uses approved Neo palette.
- Avoids excessive detail and thin fragile lines.
- Feels native to a modern mobile app.
- Transparent background.

Output:
Save as logo-mark-neo.png.

Acceptance check:
The mark should be identifiable at small app sizes, feel aligned with the style anchor, and communicate calm trust without depending on text.
```

## App Icon Source

```md
Create a square app icon source for Neo, based on the approved transparent logo mark.

App context:
Neo is an AI-powered WhatsApp commerce operating system for Nigerian SMEs that helps sellers reply customers, capture orders, verify payments, review receipts safely, coordinate delivery, manage workflows, remember customers, recover sales, and stay in control.

Style reference:
Use the approved Neo style anchor and approved logo mark. Preserve the same geometry, proportions, palette, and calm premium mood.

Composition:
Place the Neo logo mark centered on a solid deep forest green background with a subtle warm ivory or muted gold inset shape if needed for contrast. Keep the mark large enough to read at small sizes.

Requirements:
- Square 1024x1024 app icon source.
- No transparency.
- No text, letters, words, or numbers.
- No fake UI.
- No excessive detail near the edges.
- High contrast at small size.
- Keep a safe margin around the mark.

Output:
Save as app-icon-source-neo.png.

Acceptance check:
The icon should feel premium, calm, and trustworthy, and remain recognizable at small launcher sizes.
```
